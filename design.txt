Title: FlatmatesBill2
Description: An application that gets input for number of flatmates along with their checkin and checkout dates and returns the share of bill to be paid by them.
A bill has static costs and variable costs (like maintainence cost is static and utility bills are variable). Application also generate a pdf report for the quarter stating the names , number of days , share and total amount
